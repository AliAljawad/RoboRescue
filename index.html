How to Play

Join forces in our two-player game where teamwork paves the way to victory. Player 1, as the Red Adventurer, collects orange coins, while Player 2, as the Blue Explorer, gathers yellow coins. Navigate through diverse maps filled with obstacles that require cooperation to overcome. The goal is to collect all the coins on each map. Some challenges can only be tackled with the help of your teammate, so strategize and work together to succeed. Good luck!
